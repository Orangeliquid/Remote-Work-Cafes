Grandview Grind

Grandview Grind

Grandview Grind is a full traditional coffee retail house. Offering locally roasted coffee, full espresso bar, specialty drinks, smoothies, fresh bakery items and fresh fruit.

I am seeing some mixed reviews on this location. Some love the quiet atmosphere, some accuse the workers of being borderline non-verbal! I found this location to be somewhat tough to navigate seeing as the parking is lackluster and resides in a strip mall. This is the one downside, but the upside surely outweighs these shortcomings. I will let Bobby C. of Yelp.com explore this establishment further:

"I will never not recommend this place. It's cute and quaint, it's a small business that's locally owned, the baristas are friendly and great at what they do, and they have local artists' works displayed inside. It's definitely unique! Wi-Fi inside is good. There's usually seating available. It's not exceedingly loud inside. They have a nice selection of pastries. And their coffee is really good (and the beans they use rotates between different great suppliers such as Thunderkiss, Florin, Brioso, etc). I'd love to see them roast their own eventually!"
