Bexley Coffee Shop

Bexley Coffee Shop | Described by Owner

"We serve baked goods, savory quiches, cookies, and other treats that are made fresh, in-shop daily. We proudly serve locally-roasted, Thunderkiss Coffee in your choice of freshly-brewed drip, espresso-based drinks, pour-over, and cold brew. Coffee not your thing? Try one of our delicious, natural, whole-fruit smoothies, a warm cup of tea or a refreshing Italian soda."

Established in 2015.

"I opened The Bexley Coffee Shop in January 2015. I bought the shop from the previous owners and set out to make it my own by bringing in locally-roasted Thunderkiss Coffee and by making a wide variety of baked goods in-house & from scratch each day. I brightened the decor and added seating to create what I hope is a welcoming, comfortable place for people to escape the workplace, gather with friends, or settle in with a good book. I also added cheery patio seating! I am a small business owner who values our community and hope to make a positive contribution to it."

While this establishment may be on the smaller side, it doesn't diminish the potential for a determined remote worker to thrive in its cozy space. Whether you choose a seat inside or decide to bask in the open air, conducting remote work in this coffee shop is not only achievable but also enhanced by a constant stream of invigorating caffeine. The rustic blue decor envelops patrons in a comforting ambiance, evoking feelings of warmth and contentment.
